Privacy Policy — ARENA

Last updated: April 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ARENA ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ARENA (com.arenaapp.jogos).

By using ARENA, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

ARENA does not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

We may collect the following information automatically:

  • Game data: scores, ELO ratings, game statistics, in-app currency, and card collections — stored locally on your device.
  • Device information: device type, operating system version, and app version — collected by third-party services for analytics and advertising purposes.
  • Advertising data: advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID) used by Google AdMob to serve ads.
  • Purchase data: records of in-app purchases processed by Google Play Billing. We do not store payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game experience
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To process in-app purchases through Google Play
  • To analyse app usage and improve performance
  • To maintain leaderboards and game rankings

3. Third-Party Services

ARENA uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. AdMob may use your advertising ID to show personalised ads. Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — for processing in-app purchases. Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics for Firebase — for app analytics. Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Advertising

ARENA shows advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalised based on your interests using your advertising ID. You can opt out of personalised advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation

Users who purchase the "Full Access" or "Premium Pack" in-app purchases will not see advertisements.

5. In-App Purchases

ARENA offers optional in-app purchases. All purchases are processed securely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information. Purchases can be restored through Google Play.

6. Data Storage

Most game data (scores, coins, cards, settings) is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data is not transmitted to our servers. If you uninstall the app, this data will be deleted.

7. Children's Privacy

ARENA is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights (GDPR — European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure — you can request that we delete your data.
  • Right to object — you can object to the processing of your data for advertising purposes.
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

9. Your Rights (CCPA/CPRA — California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about you.
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed and to whom.
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

ARENA does not sell your personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at the email address below.

10. Data Retention

Game data stored locally on your device is retained until you uninstall the app. Advertising data managed by Google AdMob is subject to Google's retention policies.

11. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.
